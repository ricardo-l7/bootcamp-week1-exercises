Glad you came to visit my "Lifting Page"! I started my lifting journey during quarantine in March 2020. I actually didn't initiate it, my friends did. They texted me one day while they were bored at home and said "we should start lifting in your garage during quarantine," and so it began the very next day. I am very grateful that my friends got me into lifting, it has made me more disciplined and made me feel healthier. The table to the right shows my "maxes" (the most amount of weight I can do for 1 rep) for three different exercises: squat, deadlift, bench. As you can tell, my maxes went down from Summer '20 to Winter '21. This was due to my Hawai'i trip (I didn't lift at all for three months). Enjoy!
